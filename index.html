What is IndabaX?

Deep Learning IndabaX is a locally-organized Indaba that helps build a sense of connection and community among deep learning and artificial intelligence practitioners.

In 2026, Egypt hosts its most ambitious chapter yet, focusing on scalable AI solutions, ethical machine learning, and fostering local talent to compete on the global stage.
